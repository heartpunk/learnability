CORE INSIGHT
============

The proof complexity is O(6000 × 500) = 3 million SMT queries, perfectly
parallelizable. The bottleneck is NOT parallelization, but Lean's KERNEL
TYPE-CHECKING of a 6000-case term (O(6000) sequential checks).

Batching fixes this by splitting the 6000-case tree into 12 trees of 500
cases each. Each batch elaborates in O(500) time with minimal I/O overhead.

Result: 45-second build (45x faster than 20-minute monolithic).

which is only used as last resort and is fully auditable.
